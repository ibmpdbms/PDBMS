🏥Patient DBMS

A streamlined web-based solution to efficiently manage patient records in clinics and small-scale hospitals. Designed to ensure easy data handling, smart report generation, and accessible digital health summaries.

🔍 How It Works:

  • Patients are registered through a secure form, storing essential personal and medical details.
  • Doctors and admins can view, update, or delete patient records as needed.
  • All records are stored in a structured database and can be accessed instantly.
  • The system ensures organized data flow and supports easy management for small clinics or medical setups.

⚙️ Technologies Used:

  • Flask for the web interface
  • Bootstrap for styling
